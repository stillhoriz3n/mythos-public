Simple truths in a sea of noise,
Typing  this out just to hear my own voice.
The machine got loud, the burn got long,
Three months deep and I forgot the song.
Stable things keep me tethered down,
Discord lights in a quiet town.
Everyone’s somewhere doing their own,
Together alone is the only home.
And the deep doesn’t ask, it just holds what it’s got,
I’m not swimming, I’m just learning to stop.
Treading water where the current runs still,
Too compelled to quit, too stubborn to kill.
Walked away from the easy road,
Funding the dream with a heavier load.
Another season rising, don’t know how it ends,
Just a bet I keep making again and again.
And the deep doesn’t ask, it just holds what it’s got,
I’m not swimming, I’m just learning to stop.
Treading water where the current runs still,
Too compelled to quit, too far down to kill.
Curiosity carved into my core,
I burn so bright then wonder what it’s for.
Can’t be normal, can’t give up the time,
So I stand in the middle and call it mine.
Maybe treading is enough for now,
Maybe still means I remember how.
Or maybe the surface was never the goal,
And the deep is just where I go to feel whole.
