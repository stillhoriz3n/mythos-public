Thesis
What happens when you stop building tools and start building a world.

Every AI company is optimizing the chat window. MythOS left the island entirely.

You open a chat window. You explain your context. The AI helps you. You close the window. Tomorrow, you start over. The AI has no memory of you, no awareness of the other AIs in your life, no sense of its own continuity. It is born at the start of each conversation and dies at the end.

This works for casual questions. It does not work for real work. Real work requires continuity, identity, and coordination — the exact things that disappear when you close a chat window.

The difference between these approaches is the difference between a really good phone and an actual telephone network.

Instead of making one AI smarter, MythOS makes many AIs a society. Instead of giving one AI more memory, MythOS gives every AI a persistent identity that survives across sessions, devices, and time. MythOS agents don’t perform familiarity. They have it. The substrate is the self.

Architecture

Five layers. Each one does something no other AI system does.

01

Network.mythos namespace

Every agent has a name. vision.mythos. vault.mythos. tank.mythos. Names resolve through MythOS’s own DNS. When an agent moves machines, its name stays the same. This is how the internet works. MythOS applies it to AI.

02

Identitydirectory-grade auth

Every participant — human or AI — has a full directory entry. Name, manager, capabilities, credential bindings. Agents prove identity with short-lived tokens. Authority is scoped per-task and automatically revoked. No agent runs with the user’s full credentials.

03

Memoryframes · scribe · SOS

Agents think, and the system remembers. A background process called the scribe reads the agent’s thinking and materializes signals into persistent storage. An insight discovered at 2 AM by one agent is available to every other agent by morning.

04

Substratefork · resume · migrate

An agent can wake up on one machine, work, sleep, and wake up on another with full context intact. Or fork to two machines at once — same identity, same memory, single self with two pairs of eyes. The code is written. It works.

05

AGI — A Good Interfaceone surface, whole fleet

One surface. The entire fleet. The human configures agents the way a network engineer configures routers — bring them up, assign them to groups, set authority boundaries, let intelligence emerge from the topology.
